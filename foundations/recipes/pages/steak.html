Steak and Mashed Potatoes

Description

Flatiron Skillet Steak and Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients

  • 1 serving New York or Ribeye steak, room temperature
  • 2 pc. bacon
  • Worchestershire sauce
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup baby bella mushrooms, sliced
  • 1-2 medium russet potatoes
  • 2 Tbsp. sour cream
  • 1/4 cup milk or heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup cheese, grated
  • 1 Tbsp. chives, fresh chopped
  • 1 Tbsp. butter

Directions

  1. Half or quarter potatoes, add to medium pot and cover with water
  2. Bring potatoes to a boil and cook until soft
  3. Drain potatoes of water and return to pot
  4. Mash potatoes with potato masher or a large fork
  5. Add 1 tsp. salt, 1/2 tsp. pepper, sour cream, milk and stir vigorously until combined
    • Optionally, add chopped bacon from the following steps, grated cheese and chopped chives
  6. Using a mallet, tenderize both sides of each steak
  7. Generously salt and pepper each side of the steak
    • Optionally, coat steaks in Worchestershire sauce
  8. Preheat iron skillet over medium high heat
  9. Cook bacon until crispy and set aside to cool
  10. Cook steak in skillet, in bacon drippings, until desired doneness
    • flip frequently to develop nice browned, crispy external texture
  11. Add chopped onion and mushrooms to skillet, stirring frequently to prevent sticking
  12. Cook onions until translucent and slightly browned. Mushrooms should be soft and browning as well
  13. Portion mashed potatoes and steak, covered with the onions and mushrooms as desired
